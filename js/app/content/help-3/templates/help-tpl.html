Change Category Layout

There are two different layout options available for category landing pages. One showcases three products per row, with large images, while the other has four products per row and smaller images. In general, online shoppers tend to prefer larger images. We encourage you to try both layout options to see which fits your storeâ€™s aesthetic and products best.
Option 1: Large Image Category Layout; 3 products per row. (RECOMMENDED - more appealing to customers)
Option 2: Small Image Category Layout; 4 products per row.
Go to Merchandising, then Category Settings.
Select your preference from the drop-down menu called Category Page Layout.
Click the Save button at the top right corner of the page.
