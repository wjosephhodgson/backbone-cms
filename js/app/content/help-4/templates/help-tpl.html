Update My Site Logo

A logo is a surefire way to create brand awareness and differentiate your business from others. We highly encourage you to incorporate your shop's logo onto your website to help increase recognition and build your presence online.
Your logo will always live on the upper left hand corner of your eFlorist site. You can upload it through eFlorist Site Administration Tool following the easy steps below.
Go to More, then Site Theme.
Scroll down to where it says Your Logo, then click Select.
You will see a box with the heading Select an Image. Click + Upload Image.
Find the logo image you would like to use from within your computer's files. Select it, then click Open.
The logo image you selected should now appear in the Select an Image box.
Click on the image you uploaded. A yellow border will appear to confirm your selection.
Click the green Select button.
The image you uploaded should be visible in the Your Logo area.
Click the Save Changes button at the top right hand corner of the page.
Click See It Live to see what your new logo looks like on your Storefront. It may take up to an hour for the logo to display, so please keep checking if needed.
