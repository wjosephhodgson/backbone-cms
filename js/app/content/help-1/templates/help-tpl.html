Update Site Theme and Template

Your eFlorist website comes with an array of visuals to help enhance the overall shopping experience. We've designed stunning options for backgrounds, color schemes, templates and more -- all of which empower you to create the digital storefront that perfectly suits your needs.
Go to More and click Site Theme.
Under Theme Selection, click the Back button.
Use the scroll bar to view all of the Layout Options available to you.
Click on your favorite theme. Once you've clicked, you can select from available color options for that theme.
Click the Save Changes button at the top right-hand side of the page.
